How to the use the Comment Rating Tool!

In this section, you will review a code snippet and a comment below the code, provide feedback based on specific criteria. Please follow the instructions below:

How to Use the Comment Comparison Tool

  1. Review the two comments presented under "Comment 1" and "Comment 2" for the provided method below.

  3. Choose which comment you prefer by selecting and In the "Why?" section, briefly explain the reason for your preference. This helps us understand your decision-making process.
  4. Once you've made your selection and provided your reasoning, you have three options:
    • Click "Submit" to record your choice and continue to the next comparison.
    • Click "Skip" if you're unsure or don't want to compare these particular comments.
    • Click "Submit & Finish" if this is your last comparison and you want to end the session.

Remember, there are no right or wrong answers. We're interested in your honest opinion about which comment you find more effective or helpful.
